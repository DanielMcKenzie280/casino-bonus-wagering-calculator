Bonus wagering requirements explain how many times a player must wager a bonus before withdrawing winnings.

Example:

Bonus amount: $100  
Wagering requirement: 35x

Total wagering required:

100 × 35 = 3500

This means the player must place bets totaling $3500 before the bonus and winnings can be withdrawn.

Game contribution rules may vary:

Slots: 100%  
Table games: 10%  
Live casino: 0%
